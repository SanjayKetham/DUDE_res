Our Story

Passion For Great Food

Nestled in the heart of Huzurnagar, MR DUDE A/C Family Restaurant has been serving authentic Hyderabadi biryanis and delectable dishes to families for years. Every grain of rice, every spice blend is a testament to our commitment to taste and quality.

Our chefs bring the royal traditions of Hyderabadi cuisine to your table — slow-cooked Dum biryanis, crispy starters, and fresh rice dishes that will keep you coming back for more.
